## Personal Info

## Education
- **Northeastern University** (college degree)
- **French Culinary Institute** (pastry diploma + bread-making diploma)
- **Paris Training:** Studied under **Pierre Herme**, **Francois Payard**, and **Laurent Duchene**

## Career Timeline
1. Graduated from Northeastern + French Culinary Institute
2. Moved to Paris to train under master pastry chefs
3. **Age 26** — opened her own bakery in Paris: **"Les Sucreries de Melodie"** (16th arrondissement)
   - Brought the rainbow cake to Paris for the first time
   - Created the **first-ever edible cupcake dress** for Salon du Chocolat (Paris + London) — with fashion label Freyagushi
   - TV appearances: **Top Chef France**, M6, TF1, 100% Mag
   - Hundreds of press articles
   - Corporate events for **Sephora, Benefit, The Body Shop, British Airways**
   - Bakery later closed due to health issues
4. **2X Food Network CHOPPED Champion** (USA)
5. **October 2018** — Founded **Bake It with Mel** (curated baking kits)
6. **April 16, 2024** — Published debut cookbook
7. Currently: Private pastry chef for celebrities, bakery consultant, content creator, educator
