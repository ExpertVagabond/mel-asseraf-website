About the Book

Chef Mel demystifies French techniques and shows you how to use them, along with ten basic ingredients, to make everything from simple cookies, biscotti, bars, and the best brownies you've ever tasted, to impressive French pastries and celebratory desserts.

With 100 recipes, clear and concise instructions, handy chef's tips, and information on everything you need in your baking arsenal, Sweet Treats from Brownies to Brioche is the only baking book you'll ever need.

A Note from Chef Mel

This book is my love letter to baking. Every recipe in these pages comes from years of training in Paris, late nights perfecting ratios, and the joy of watching someone take their first bite of a perfectly baked pastry.

I wrote Sweet Treats to prove that you don't need a pastry degree or a professional kitchen to create something extraordinary. With just ten ingredients and the right technique, anyone can bake like a chef.

— Mel
