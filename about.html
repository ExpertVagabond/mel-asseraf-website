Meet Mel

Chef Melodie "Mel" Asseraf fell in love with baking at a young age. After graduating from Northeastern University and earning her pastry diploma from the French Culinary Institute, she set out on a journey that would take her from New York City to the heart of Paris.

In Paris, Mel trained under some of the greatest pastry masters of our time — Pierre Hermé, François Payard, and Laurent Duchêne. Their teachings in classical French technique became the foundation of everything she creates.

Today, with over 15 years of expertise, Mel is a two-time Food Network Chopped Champion, published cookbook author, and the founder and CEO of Bake It with Mel — sharing her passion for pastry with a community of hundreds of thousands.
